Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay in advance?

No. You have to pay half of the payment in advance and the rest half you have to pay by the end of the project.

How long will it take to get the final design?

Generally, we provide the project within a month except the budget planning. However, it depends on the size and decorations.

Does Rinterio provide interior design?

We are proud to provide the complete design of a building including both the exterior and interior.

How advance should I book a design for my apartment?

Typically, it is better to book your order at least 4-6 months before you want the final layout.

Is the payment refundable if I change my decision?

Although we return a significant portion of the payment if our client don't want the design, we keep 20% of the full amount as service charge.
